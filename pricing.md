# Pricing — AI Residency

## Engagement model
- Specialist embedded in your team for 4-12 weeks
- Onsite, hybrid, or fully remote (you choose)
- One-week trial: full refund or replacement if it isn't working
- No retainers, no lock-in
- 2 weeks notice to cancel after the trial week

## Rates
- From AUD $30 per hour
- Exact rate depends on specialist seniority and length of placement
- Typical 4-week sprint: approximately AUD $4,800-$9,600 depending on seniority and hours
- Currency: AUD (Australia only)

## Conversion to full-time
- If you convert a specialist to a full-time hire, a one-time recruitment-style placement fee applies
- Fee quoted on request
